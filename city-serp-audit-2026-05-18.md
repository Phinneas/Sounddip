## What This Means for Sounddip

**The directory column is zero across all five cities.** This is the entire thesis confirmed in live data.

The only multi-practitioner results in any city are Eventbrite, ClassBento, and Letsbatch — general activity booking platforms that happen to list sound baths alongside cooking classes and axe-throwing. None of them:
- Filter by sound bath format
- Show practitioner credentials
- Surface pricing data
- Let you hear a sample
- Distinguish spiritual from secular sessions
- Help a first-timer choose between practitioners

The three listicles (LA Times, modernhypno, Atlanta Magazine) are the closest thing to curation — but they're static editorial pieces from 2023–2024. None link to booking. None are structured.

**Priority launch markets based on editorial gap:**
1. **Austin** — No listicle, no dominant studio, event-platforms are the only aggregators. First mover wins.
2. **Chicago** — No listicle at all. A "Best Sound Baths in Chicago" city page from Sounddip would immediately be the best editorial resource in the market.
3. **NYC** — No listicle but competitive individual studios. Official Ritual and Soundawn both have SEO presence; a directory would need to aggregate them rather than compete with them.
4. **Atlanta** — Atlanta Magazine has the listicle but doesn't link to booking. A structured page outcompetes a static article.
5. **LA** — Most competitive (LA Times + modernhypno hold positions). Still no directory. Hardest to break into editorially but highest search volume.
